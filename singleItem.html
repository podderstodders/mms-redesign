Black Mirror Project (BLACKMIRROR)

by David Jonathan

R: $39.95

Difficulty: Easy

From the best-selling creator of VOX and Synergy, David Jonathan is back with more powerful mentalism using the most common and relatable object in the world: your phone. Black Mirror Project has been in development for over a year and features SEVEN mind melting effects that are a must have for any mentalist!

A stunning rendition using the genuine Books app. You hand them the phone; they swipe to a random page in a book and remember a word they see. They can even hand the phone to a friend who swipes to another page and also remembers a word. Without ever retrieving the phone and with nothing spoken or written down, you instantly reveal the thought of words! It uses no photos; screen recordings or special books and they are always looking at the genuine pages in the Books app!

Six lotto numbers are freely named, which perfectly match the winning numbers on a photo of a lotto ticket in your phone's photo gallery! This uses a clever combination of methods that make this impossible, stunning effect a reality!

A test conditions, digital equivalent of a center tear with a locked phone. You will love this sneaky, bold peek!

Predict a thought of object or animal in your photo gallery, or even as your wallpaper, without ever touching the phone!

An item is freely chosen from a list of 30 birthday gift ideas. In your gallery is an album titled "Your Gift" with just one photo inside, and it's of the gift they chose! Not only that, but the date also that photo was taken astoundingly happens to be their actual birthday!

A free choice of any city is revealed to have been predicted in Google Maps without ever touching the phone.

A destination is chosen from a list of the Top 20 travel hotspots across the world and also any date is named for when they would like to travel. Impossibly, you knew exactly what they would choose as earlier you had placed a photo in your gallery that is undeniable proof, they would pick that location! You show them it's a generic photo of a beach - which seemingly could have fit ANY of the destinations from the list. However, you reveal the geo-tag of the photo to show it really was taken at their chosen location! Not only that, but the date of the photo is also the exact date they had freely named! (Please note this effect requires iOS 17 or later).

  • Not a magic app - uses native features available from Apple in iOS.
  • Incredibly easy to perform - can be done by any skill level!
  • Every effect ends clean.
  • Compatible with iOS devices running iOS 13+ (iOS 17+ is recommended).
  • Not supported for Android devices (sorry).
  • No language restrictions.
  • Multiple variations of each effect are taught.
  • Setup is taught in crystal clear detail with over 3.5 hours of video instruction.
  • Certain effects require an internet connection to perform while others work offline.
  • Every routine can be performed with just your phone, though optional handlings are also taught that use any inexpensive Bluetooth selfie remote for certain routines.
  • Supports integrations with popular apps including Inject, QR Genie & Hydra.

Black Mirror Project includes all of the digital components and assets, video tutorials, a detailed PDF guide and exclusive access to a private Facebook group.

Once your phone is equipped with these mesmerizing routines, you will be ready to absolutely destroy audiences at any time!

This is a PHYSICAL version - you will be shipped a pamphlet that contains the secrets of how to perform BLACK MIRROR PROJECT.
